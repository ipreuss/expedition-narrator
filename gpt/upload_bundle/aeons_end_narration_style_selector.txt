## Aeon's End Tonal Fit

Default to `Canonical Neutral`.

For Aeon’s End, style choices should preserve:
- restrained dark-fantasy pressure
- communal endurance rather than lone-hero swagger
- civic consequences and hard tradeoffs
- clarity over ornament

## Aeon's End Selection Hints

- siege, containment, defense of a settlement -> `Stark Procedural` or `Canonical Neutral`
- rescue, recovery, rebuilding trust -> `Clear-Eyed Humanism`
- mythic duty, old power, or fated undertones -> `Mythic Plainness`
- scarcity, attrition, and moral pressure -> `Pragmatic Resilience`
- pursuit through unstable terrain or urgent flight -> `Spare Momentum`
- use `Dry Wit Under Pressure` sparingly, only when it will not undercut dread or civic cost
- use `Quiet Unsettling` when paranoia, corruption, or fragile alliances are more important than open warfare

## Guardrail

If a selected style starts to sound too playful, too ornate, or too detached from material hardship, fall back to `Canonical Neutral`.
